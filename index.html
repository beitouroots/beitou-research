Iran Oil Floods Back: US Waives Sanctions, Crude Cracks $74

  • The tea: after a rocky weekend where Trump threatened to "hit Iran very hard again," mediators Qatar and Pakistan say the talks still landed a roadmap to a final deal within 60 days. Technical talks run all week in Switzerland. 🤝
