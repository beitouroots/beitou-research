Core PCE Friday Is the Wall the Rally Has to Climb

  • The setup: the Warsh-led Fed held at 3.50-3.75% on June 17 and leaned hard on price stability. The median 2026 dot was lifted to 3.8%, which now points to a hike rather than a cut. 🦅

Micron Just Turned an AI Story Into a Signed Contract

AI's Memory Crunch Just Hit Your Wallet
